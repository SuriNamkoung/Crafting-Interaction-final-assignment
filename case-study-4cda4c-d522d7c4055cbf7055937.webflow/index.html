Situation

The impact of the global pandemic in 2020 has changed the world in innumerable ways. The travel and tourism industry now finds itself in a unique moment in time to address some of the greatest systemic challenges caused by the industry’s acceleration in recent years, and to take the opportunity to create a more sustainable future for destinations, communities, and travelers alike. This can include protecting wildlife, preserving the environment, tourism growth, thriving communities

On-boarding

Activate Greengle Extension to support your sustainable trip

After downloading Greengle, users can activate extension on Google map so that it acts as a Google map's green mode. Users deactivate Greengle when they just want to use normal version of Google map.

Search

Search sustainable places

Users can search sustainable places including accommodation, restaurants, attractions, In the detail page, criteria to check how much the location keep sustainability will be displayed. All the reviews and ratings are based on sustainability , which can give credible information to travelers.

Explore & Go

Compare sustainable transportation with less carbon emission

Users can compare what kinds of transportation will be more helpful for the earth by checking the average carbon footprint when their decisions are facilitated. Greengle bookmark category will placed so that travelers can saved sustainable destination in Google map.

Impact

Check your effort to save the earth

Since users allow location during on-boarding, they can get data about how much they contribute themselves on responsible travel. Users can check their carbon footprint and compare the one from average travelers', How much they cared about the environment compared to the average traveler can be checked through easy-to-understand data visualization using metaphors in real life. And the Greengle level system allow users to keep taking responsible actions toward environment.

Reward

Get reward coming from your sustainable actions

There are some missions for sustainable people that travelers can take. Users can get points when accomplishing the missions and accumulated points will let them get discount on sustainable brands such as Freitag, Patagonia, and etc.
